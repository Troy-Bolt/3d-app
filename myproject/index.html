üìú Manifesto

Hello and thank you for visiting my page. My name is Troy Bolt, and I am building a career for myself as a Web Developer. My journey started during High School where became interested in Information Technology, the Internet of Things (IoT), and Website Design & Development. I went on to study Computer Science in College and continued my learning in my personal time and during a valuable work placement. I would like to build beautiful and useful websites for a wide variety of individuals and companies looking to improve their online presence. My knowledge is forever growing, but I can proudly say I am familiar with multiple languages and frameworks.

I mainly use the core languages for Front-end Web Development of HTML, CSS, Python and Javascript, and have experience using frameworks and libraries such as React and Next.js. I also confidently use common software such as Photoshop and Godot, and online programmes such as Figma during the design process. My favourite part of Web Design & Development is seeing the finished product online, where everyone in the world has access to it, but I thoroughly enjoy the whole process, from understanding a client's unique requirements and planning the usability, through wire framing and designing, to deploying the finished code onto a server ready to go live. If you would like a website built, or you're looking to hire a coder, please contact me.
